About Anand Jewellers

Crafting Timeless Elegance Since 2001

Founded in 2001, Anand Jewellers has grown into a household name in handcrafted gold, silver, and diamond jewelry. Every piece is a blend of heritage craftsmanship and contemporary design.

We specialize in exquisite collections across Gold, Silver, Diamond, Platinum, and Precious Gemstones—including Rings, Earrings, Mangalsutras, Necklaces, Bangles, Anklets, and more.

As a government-approved gold valuer, Anand Jewellers is recognized for precision, authenticity, and integrity in all evaluations. Our strong partnerships with over 10 leading banks across 40+ branches allow us to provide specialized services for bank loan processing, asset-backed financing, and VISA-related documentation.

We take pride in our authenticity, quality, and customer-first values that keep clients coming back for generations.
